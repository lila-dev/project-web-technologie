About this Course

Marketing, or mercatique, is a set of sales techniques and their implementation. Marketing should not be confused with marketing management, often called “marketing” for convenience, but which refers to the management of the complex set of methodologies enabling the organization to be and remain competitive in a world moving competitive. This organizational culture has two fundamental dimensions: the customers (in the broad sense) and the company, dimensions that it must seek to take into account simultaneously in a balanced way.
